Homemade Burger

A Delicious Homemade Burger Recipe!

ingredients

  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 1 hamburger bun
  • 1 slice of cheese
  • 1 lettuce leaf
  • 1 slice of tomato
  • 1 slice of onion
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Season the ground beef with salt and pepper, then shape it into a patty.
  2. Cook the patty in a pan over medium heat for about 3-4 minutes per side.
  3. Place a slice of cheese on top of the patty and let it melt.
  4. Toast the hamburger bun lightly.
  5. Assemble the burger by placing the patty on the bottom bun, followed by lettuce, tomato, and onion.
  6. Top with the other half of the bun and serve immediately.
